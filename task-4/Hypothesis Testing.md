# Hypothesis Testing – Step 1

# Objective:
The objective of this step is to define a testable hypothesis based on the video game sales dataset.

# Hypothesis:
Games with higher critic scores tend to achieve higher total sales.

# Explanation:
This hypothesis assumes that games receiving better reviews from critics are more likely to attract players and generate higher sales. Critic scores may influence customer decisions, thereby impacting the commercial success of a game.

This hypothesis will be tested in the next step using statistical methods to determine whether a significant relationship exists between critic scores and total sales.
